Meet The Team

Adriel Lettman

Developer

Hey! I am the developer and it has been a blast making and being apart of the team. I was a student at UMBC home of the retrievers. I never thought I would be a programmer but once I started I couldn't stop. When my friend told me about this idea he wanted to do I had to join. It sounded like it would be a lot of fun and it would be a great place to start for me. It took a lot of work but here you are! Your first wash is on me :)
Paley C.

CEO

Hey! I am the CEO and also a detailer. I sweat the details on every job that I do. The most important thing to me is making sure that you get the best possible job at the best possible price in the fastest time. But I saved the best for last! I come to you. You never have to go to another car wash again. None of the industrial brushes that don't give your car the attention it deserves. We are the total detail.
Man N.

Graphic Designer

I have been jumping around from place to place doing designs but I really wanted to settle down. I wanted to be apart of something that is positive for the community but where I could also have fun. That's when I met the Detailers team. From the first conversation I knew this was a chance I could not pass up and I took it. Best decision of my life. The work is fulfilling and seeing the looks on your faces when see your shiny car make me feel so good!
